# Tiempo profundo

## Bibliographic Summary

*Tiempo profundo*. H&O Editorial, 244 pp., €22. Original Spanish text by [Ana Gorría](../authors/gorria_ana.md). Published 2025. [date uncertain — verify exact publication date]

## Synopsis

A double movement: the reconstruction of Ana Gorría's family memory (grandmother, father, house, neighbourhood, dog, siblings) through the family photo album, combined with essay-studies on memory's functioning through "very vivid and precise" comments on the work of more than a dozen female photographers. The photo album serves both as register and Ouija board — summoning voices that never fully pass in memory. (Source: [Torné 2025](../sources/2025-07-31_tiempo-profundo-gorria.md))

## Critical Overview

[Gonzalo Torné's 2025 review](../sources/2025-07-31_tiempo-profundo-gorria.md) opens by identifying *Tiempo profundo* as a book that follows neither commercial literature nor creative writing school conventions and fits no evident genre — a species of book deserving maximum critical attention. He calls it "delicate and wise," offering the pleasure of pages where "what will come next is never predictable" — not through narrative intrigue but through the deployment of literary imagination.

The essay sections are "first-category"; the emotional wingspan opens in the passages reconstructing family fragments, losses, and absences with the help of a photo album that functions as "as much a register as a Ouija board." Torné develops the book's poetics of discontinuous family memory — fragile compared to official history but "more intense and sensitive" — with a comparison to the palaeontologist reconstructing a dinosaur from a jaw fragment.

The review closes on the book's paradox: looking at the album attenuates the years — not a return to childhood (the eyes preserve accumulated experience) but the maturing of consciousness alongside an ageing body.
