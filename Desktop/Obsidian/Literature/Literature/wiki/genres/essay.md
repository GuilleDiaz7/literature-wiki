# Essay

## Definition

Non-fictional prose argument or reflection. Distinguished from the novel by its non-fictional contract with the reader; distinguished from documentary forms (diary, letters) by its rhetorical and argumentative intention. 9 works in this wiki.

## Subgenres in This Wiki

### Essay (discursive / argumentative)
