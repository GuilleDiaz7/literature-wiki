# Documentary Forms

## Definition

Non-fictional writings not structured as argument (which would be essay) but as record: the letter, the diary, the dream journal. Their value is testimonial and archival as much as literary. The author's intention is not primarily rhetorical. 4 works in this wiki.

## Subgenres in This Wiki

### Letters / Epistolary
Correspondence between two or more parties, published as a collection. The literary interest lies in the dialogue, the historical moment, and what is revealed obliquely.

### Diary / Journal
A record kept over time by a single author, not written for publication. The temporality — the not-knowing what comes next — is constitutive of the form.
