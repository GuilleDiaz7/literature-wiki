# El movimiento del cuerpo a través del espacio

## Bibliographic Summary

*The Motion of the Body Through Space* (2018). Reviewed by Torné in July 2023. Published in Spanish translation.

## Synopsis

A couple in their sixties: the husband, having recently retired, becomes obsessed with extreme physical fitness — training for a triathlon in the teeth of physical deterioration. The novel is a satire of the contemporary body-cult: the moralizing of exercise, the displacement of deeper anxieties onto physical self-punishment, the competitive logic of wellness culture. The wife's perspective provides the ironic counterpoint.

## Critical Overview

[Torné](../critics/torne_gonzalo.md): solid satirical work but below *Tenemos que hablar de Kevin* in ambition and emotional stakes. The satirical target (body-cult, wellness culture) is well chosen and the execution is competent. But Shriver's real power — the close analysis of moral psychology under pressure — operates at a lower intensity here. The novel is more sociological than psychological.
