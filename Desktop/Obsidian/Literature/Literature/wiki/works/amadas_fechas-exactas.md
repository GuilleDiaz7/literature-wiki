# Fechas exactas de personas amadas

## Bibliographic Summary

By [Mario Amadas](../authors/amadas_mario.md), b.1986 Barcelona. Small-press publication. Reviewed by Torné in September 2023.

## Synopsis

A narrator travels to Ghana and reconstructs, with documentary precision, the exact dates — births, deaths, meetings, departures — of people he has loved or lost. The "exact dates" of the title function as an anti-elegiac gesture: precision as a way of honoring the specific against the general, the particular life against abstraction. The Ghana material adds an ethnographic and political dimension.

## Critical Overview

[Torné](../critics/torne_gonzalo.md): the prose is distinguished by a quality he calls precision — every word placed with care, nothing vague, emotional life rendered in exact particulars. Amadas is seriously underread: the small-press publication, the lack of major prizes, the absence from review circuits has meant that a genuinely distinctive voice reaches almost no one. Torné explicitly notes this as editorial blindness.
