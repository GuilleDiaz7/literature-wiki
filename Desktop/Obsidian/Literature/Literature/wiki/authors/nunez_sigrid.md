## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *Los vulnerables*](../sources/2024-10-01_los-vulnerables-nunez.md): Nunez's style is schematic, allusive, learned, and analytical — more interested in presenting lives as summaries and examples than in extended narration. The sparseness is purposive: concentrated on the smaller, more decisive registers of human emotion — tenderness, trust, responsibility, care. Torné compares her project to Ali Smith's, though Nunez favours vulnerability over political recuperation.
