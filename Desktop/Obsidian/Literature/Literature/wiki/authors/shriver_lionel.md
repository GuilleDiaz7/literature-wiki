# Lionel Shriver

## Biographical Context

Lionel Shriver (born Margaret Ann Shriver; Gastonia, North Carolina, 1957) is an American novelist. She adopted the name Lionel at age fifteen.

## Major Works

- *We Need to Talk About Kevin* (*Tenemos que hablar de Kevin*, 2003) — Orange Prize; her defining work; not reviewed in this wiki
- [*El movimiento del cuerpo a través del espacio* (*The Motion of the Body Through Space*, 2018)](../works/shriver_movimiento-cuerpo.md) — reviewed by Torné

## Style and Technique

Agile prose; sharp dialogue, often substituting for action; satirical intelligence. *Kevin* established a mode of cruel, lucid, malevolent fiction that her subsequent work has not quite replicated in force.

## Critical Reputation

Permanently overshadowed by *Kevin* — [Torné](../critics/torne_gonzalo.md) notes this explicitly, calling *El movimiento* a further satirical retablo in a career marked by its devastating first masterpiece. Later work is described as "malevolent and lucid and funny" but with less edge and a more relaxed tone.
