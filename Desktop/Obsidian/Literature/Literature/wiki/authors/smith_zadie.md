## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *La impostura*](../sources/2024-11-14_la-impostura-smith.md): Smith's contemporary approach to Victorian material involves two formal choices — construction from very short narrative units (almost gags or pills, compressed by humour and separated by ellipses) and a symbolic dimension requiring interpretive keys beyond the plot action. The "dancing signifier" of imposture is a device of genuine ambition. Torné's reservation: this brevity becomes repetitive and the novel closes somewhat falsely.
