## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *El Vado de los Zorros*](../sources/2025-11-12_el-vado-zorros-starobinets.md): Starobinets's approach is not magical realism (which requires a dominant realistic framework) but "exotic and eccentric historicism" — the fantastic is at the heart of the narrative from the start. Her poetics is one of *apabullamiento* — overwhelming accumulation of strange elements, diverse narrative techniques, sustained across 700+ pages. Closest literary comparandum: Pynchon, "taking his characters' eccentricities quite seriously" — but without Pynchon's political-critical ambition.
