## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *Tiempo profundo*](../sources/2025-07-31_tiempo-profundo-gorria.md): Gorría's book follows neither commercial literature conventions nor creative writing school codes and fits no evident genre — a rare independence. Torné praises the literary imagination that makes each page unpredictable not through intrigue but through genuine deployment of imaginative possibility. The essay sections are "first-category"; the memoir passages are described as emotionally expansive — fragments of stories, character profiles, recounts of losses.
