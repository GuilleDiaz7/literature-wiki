# Han Kang

## Biographical Context

Born 1970 in Gwangju, South Korea. Nobel Prize in Literature 2024. Known internationally for *La vegetariana* (*The Vegetarian*, Booker International 2016) and *Actos humanos* (*Human Acts*). The review of *Imposible decir adiós* was published shortly after the Nobel Prize announcement, situating the novel within heightened international attention. ## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *Imposible decir adiós*](../sources/2025-01-13_imposible-decir-adios-kang.md): Four formal achievements: (1) precision in describing physical and mental pain; (2) fluid passage between oneiric landscape and tactile realism, dissolving borders; (3) spiral temporal syntax — advancing by retreating, retrospective illumination as important as present events; (4) plasticity in describing nature's structure and relation to bodies. Historical violence is structurally suppressed — manifesting through dreams, somatic symptoms, blurred testimony. This formal repression is itself a literary-political choice.
