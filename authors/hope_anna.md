## Style and Technique

Per [Gonzalo Torné's review of *Albión*](../sources/2026-03-09_albion-anna-hope.md): Hope handles the standard tools of creative writing pedagogy with competence — dialogue, characterisation, description, dramatic revelation. She knows her target reader and delivers consistently from the first page. Her prose does not, in Torné's assessment, reach the ironic intelligence of Jane Austen or the dark passion of Iris Murdoch.
